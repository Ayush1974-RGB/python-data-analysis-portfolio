# 🚀 Business Intelligence & Market Analysis Dashboard

## 📖 Project Overview

This capstone project represents the culmination of my data analytics learning journey.

The project combines customer analytics, sales analytics, and real estate market analysis into a complete Business Intelligence solution. Using multiple datasets and analytical techniques, the project demonstrates how data-driven insights can support business decision-making.

The analysis covers customer retention, sales performance, market trends, statistical testing, and predictive modeling.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Perform end-to-end business analysis
* Analyze customer behavior and churn
* Evaluate sales performance
* Study real estate market trends
* Apply statistical techniques
* Generate actionable business recommendations
* Create professional dashboards and reports
